3. Recommended Pricing

Recommended Monthly Price
£3.99/month
or £29.99/year (£2.50/mo — 37% saving)
Below YNAB ($14.99/mo) · Above simple calculators (£0–5 one-time)

Rationale

  • Below YNAB — YNAB is a full-budget platform with bank sync, live support, and 1M+ users. You're not competing at their scale or cost base yet.
  • Above pure debt calculators — Your app provides significantly more value than simple debt snowball calculators through AI insights, UK tax engine, mortgage simulator, and scenario planning.
  • UK market positioning — The UK personal finance app market is underserved vs. the US. £3.99/mo is the "impulse subscribe" threshold for UK consumers.
  • AI operating costs covered — Firebase AI (Gemini) has per-request costs that a subscription model sustains.
  • Competitive with niche competitors — Undercuts OxbowSoft's subscription (their users complain it's "a little high") while offering more features.

6. Key Takeaways

Summary

  • £3.99/month (or £29.99/year) is the sweet spot for a UK-focused niche app with AI features and no established brand recognition.
  • AI Insights should be the flagship premium feature — it's unique in the market and has real operating costs.
  • Free tier limited to 2 debts provides enough value to demonstrate the app while creating clear upgrade motivation.
  • Annual discount (37%) encourages commitment and reduces churn.
  • Future price increases to £4.99–6.99 are justified once you add Open Banking integration or multi-device sync.
