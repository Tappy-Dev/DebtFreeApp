Full Description

Know your debt-free date. Own your financial future. Debt Free is a personal finance app built for UK users. Manage your budget in pounds, track debts, model mortgage overpayments, and plan ahead with AI-powered insights — all from one place. BUDGET MANAGEMENT • Set up monthly income, expenses, and bills in GBP (£) • Copy budgets between months or start fresh • Track actual spending against your budget each month • Configure your financial month to start on your pay day • Close completed months to lock in your records DEBT TRACKING & REPAYMENT • Add all your debts with balances, APR, and minimum payments • View a clear summary of total debt and monthly obligations • Supports percentage-based and fixed minimum payment types • See projected payoff dates and total interest costs REPAYMENT STRATEGIES • Simulate the Avalanche method (highest APR first) to minimise interest • Simulate the Snowball method (smallest balance first) for momentum • Compare strategies side-by-side to find what works best for you MORTGAGE OVERPAYMENT ANALYSIS • Enter your mortgage details — balance, rate, term, and monthly payment • Simulate overpayments and see how much time and interest you could save • Compare the impact of overpaying your mortgage vs paying off other debts SALARY SACRIFICE MODELLING • Model salary sacrifice contributions and their net cost impact • Understand how sacrifice schemes affect your available cash flow WHAT-IF SCENARIO PLANNER • Add future events — pay rises, one-off expenses, extra debt payments • See how life changes could impact your debt-free timeline • Analyse scenarios with AI to understand the financial impact AI FINANCIAL ADVISOR • Get personalised analysis powered by Google Gemini • Ask whether debt consolidation would save you money • Compare Avalanche vs Snowball with your actual numbers • Find out if you should build an emergency fund first • Discover where extra money has the most impact — mortgage or debts • Learn what size purchase you can absorb without derailing your plan • Advice considers UK-typical interest rates and financial products DASHBOARD & INSIGHTS • At-a-glance summary of your debts, mortgage, and monthly cash flow • See your remaining cash after all obligations • Navigate to detailed breakdowns from the dashboard PRIVACY FIRST • All financial data is stored locally on your device • No account required — no sign-ups, no cloud sync • AI insights only send a summary when you choose to ask a question • No ads, no tracking DESIGNED FOR THE UK • All amounts in GBP (£) • AI advice uses UK-relevant rates, products, and terminology • Salary sacrifice modelling reflects UK workplace schemes 🔒 Your financial data never leaves your device. No account needed. No ads. Ever.
