Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Debt Free ("the app", "we", "us") is developed by Tappy Taps Studio. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data You Enter

All financial data you enter — including debts, mortgage details, income, expenses, and budget figures — is stored locally on your device only using an encrypted SQLite database. We do not upload, transmit, or share this data with any server we operate.

2. AI Assistant

The AI Budget Assistant feature sends anonymised, aggregated financial summaries to Google Firebase AI (Vertex AI / Gemini) to generate responses. This data:

  • Does not include your name, account numbers, or directly identifying information
  • Is processed by Google under their Privacy Policy
  • Is not retained by us after the response is returned

If you do not wish to use this feature, simply do not use the Planner / AI Assistant screen.

3. Firebase Analytics & Crash Reporting

We use Google Firebase for app stability and basic usage analytics. Firebase may collect:

  • Anonymous device information (OS version, device model)
  • App crash reports and performance data
  • Basic session and event analytics (screens visited, features used)

No personal financial data is included in these reports. Firebase data is processed by Google under their Privacy Policy.

4. In-App Purchases

Subscription and purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not store or process your payment details. Google's payment policies apply.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact details
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not sync your financial data to any cloud service we control

6. Data Storage & Security

Your financial data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. Sensitive credentials (where applicable) use Android's encrypted secure storage. We recommend keeping your device secured with a screen lock.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your Rights

Since your financial data is stored only on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app or using the app's built-in clear/reset functionality. For Firebase data, you can request deletion by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
